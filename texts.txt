This will be a brief explanation

So what is Pathfinding?
Pathfinding is a set of techniques 
used to find the most efficient 
route between two points.

In this program, I am going to
illustrate how and why pathfinding
algorithms are used.

Today we are going to discuss
the A* pathfinding algorithm.

The A* pathfinding algorithm
consists of these key features:

Heuristics,
A start node,
An end node,
Obstacles,
And neighbouring nodes.

Lets take a deep dive into what 
each of these components of the
A* pathfinding algorithm do and
how they are implemented during
programming/development stages.
